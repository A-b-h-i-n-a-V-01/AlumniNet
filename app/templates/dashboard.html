About SNGCE

Sree Narayana Gurukulam College of Engineering (SNGCE) is a pioneer, self-financing educational institution established in the year 2002 to impart engineering and management education with state-of-the-art amenities. Managed by the Sree Narayana Gurukulam Charitable Trust, SNGCE has established itself as a premier destination for transformative technical education in Kerala.

Visit Official Website
{# Vision & Mission Section #}

Our Vision

Realization of a self-contained centre of excellence for value-based transformative education, research and innovation.

Our Mission

  • To be led by consistent standards, that will place the institution on a high plane of excellence in education, research, innovation and creativity.
  • To produce globally competent professionals through excellent teaching-learning process and industry interaction.
  • To transform learners into better citizens with ingrained values, knowledge, skills and aptitude to serve society.
